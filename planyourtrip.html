  • What type of Power Adapters will I need?

    Power outlets are 120 volts (the same as in the United States).

  • What Language do Tanitian's speak?

    While many younger Tanitians speak fluent English. Very little English is spoken in rural areas, especially by the older residents who speak our own dialect of Tanitianese

  • What is the legal drinking age in Taniti?

    The drinking age on Taniti is 18 and the drinking age is not strictly enforced. It is important to note that Alcohol is not allowed to be served or sold between the hours of midnight and 9:00 a.m.
